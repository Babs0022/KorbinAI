Our Unfair Advantages

1. Proactive, Goal-Oriented AI: While competitors are reactive, KorbinAI is designed to be a proactive partner that understands and executes complex, high-level goals. This is our core differentiator.

3. Developer-First Approach: With features like automated sitemap generation and a focus on code-aware AI, we are uniquely positioned to serve the needs of developers who are also creators.

What to Build Next
